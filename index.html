CargolandFood

CargolandFood is a complete multi-vendor food delivery ecosystem built with modern web and mobile technologies. It connects customers, restaurants, and riders in real-time, providing a seamless and scalable food delivery experience.

Project Description

CargolandFood provides an all-in-one delivery ecosystem with apps for customers, restaurants, and riders. It features real-time tracking, secure payments, referral programs, and a powerful admin dashboard for managing operations at scale.

The platform was designed to handle high traffic, ensure seamless ordering, and improve customer satisfaction while giving restaurants and delivery partners the tools to grow their businesses.

The Story

The vision behind CargolandFood was to empower food businesses with a reliable platform while providing customers with convenience and riders with fair opportunities. The system was developed iteratively with constant feedback from stakeholders to ensure it meets real-world needs.

Our Approach

We adopted a modular and scalable architecture, integrating advanced technologies such as Laravel for backend, Flutter/React Native for mobile, and real-time notifications via Firebase/Twilio. A focus was placed on UI/UX to ensure smooth onboarding and user engagement across all apps.
